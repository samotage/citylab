# Your energy is trapped at your meter

Four million Australian households generate their own electricity. But every kilowatt-hour they produce is locked to one address.

Your solar earns value — but only at home. Your battery arbitrages — but only at home. Step off the property and that value disappears.

# Follow Me Power

Your energy follows you — wherever you plug in, wherever you consume, wherever you need power.

Your home solar generates credits. Those credits settle against any grid connection point in the country, through citEther's settlement layer.

> **The grid is the wire. citEther is the settlement.**

Your energy account is no longer tied to your meter. It is tied to you.

# Auto-Arb + Community

<div class="columns">

**The Auto-Arb Engine**

Set-and-forget orchestration of every smart asset in your home. Battery, HVAC, hot water, EV, pool pump.

Charges when cheap, discharges when valuable, pre-cools before a spike, never sacrifices comfort. You set the rules, Auto-Arb follows them.

# The economics are honest

The grid is not free. When your energy travels from your roof to your mum's flat, there are transmission and distribution charges — third-party network fees for using the wires.

citEther accounts for these. The consumer sees net value after network costs.

<div class="columns">

**Still beats zero FiT**

Even after network fees, Follow Me Power pays better than a zero-cent feed-in tariff — because the alternative is exporting for nothing.

**Revenue model**

citEther takes a margin on net value created. DNSPs pay for avoided network upgrades. When energy stays within a Pod, network charges shrink — electrons never leave the local loop.

</div>
